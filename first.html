Daraz

Daraz is a leading online marketplace in South Asia, headquartered in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
Founded in 2012, it operates in Bangladesh, Pakistan, Nepal, Sri Lanka, and Myanmar. Daraz provides a platform for third-party sellers to list and sell their products, and handles the logistics and payments for these transactions.
